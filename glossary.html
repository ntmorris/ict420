Glossary

1 Sulla Consul of Rome as of the mentioned civil war
2 Marius Former Consul of Rome. Enacted the famous 'Marian Reforms'. Uncle of Caesar by marriage.
3 Aegean Sea The sea between modern Greece ("Achaea") and Turkey ("Asia")
4 Military Tribune Equivalent to a modern middle-ranking officer. Tribunes commanded portions of the army, but were subordinates of those appointed by the senate. This position was often held by young senatorial hopefuls.
5 Quaestor A supervisor of the Roman Republic's treasury and finances.
6 Curule Aedile Aediles maintained public holdings and regulated public festivals. Curule Aediles were one of two distinctions of Aedile, one open to both commoners and nobility.
7 Pontifex Maximus The Chief High Priest of Rome. The highest position of Roman religion.
8 Catilinarian Conspiracy The Second Catilinarian Conspiracy was a conspiracy by Catiline (Catilina) to overthrow the consulship of Cicero. Caesar was accused of being a member.
